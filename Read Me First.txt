Stick-It — Read This First
===========================

HOW TO OPEN STICK-IT (one-time thing, then never again)

1. Drag Stick-It.app into your Applications folder.

2. Double-click it to open.

3. macOS will block it and say it can't verify the developer.
   This is expected — click Done.

4. Open System Settings, then go to Privacy & Security.

5. Scroll all the way to the bottom of that page.

6. You'll see a line: "Stick-It" was blocked to protect your Mac.
   Click Open Anyway next to it, then confirm with your
   password or Touch ID.

Stick-It will open. Every time after this, it opens normally —
no more warnings, no more steps.
