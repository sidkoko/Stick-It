Stick-It — Read This First
===========================

If macOS shows a warning when you open Stick-It saying it can't verify
the developer, that's expected — this app isn't in the App Store (yet),
so your Mac double-checks the first time.

FASTEST FIX — paste this into Terminal, then press Return:

    xattr -cr "/Applications/Stick-It.app"

Then open Stick-It normally.

Prefer not to use Terminal? Full click-by-click steps here:
https://claude.ai/code/artifact/0f0214ff-8904-4aa5-a158-0fab4f0fe7f5

Either way, this only happens once — every time after, it opens instantly.
