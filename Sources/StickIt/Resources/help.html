📝 Stick-It

Sticky notes that don't make you guess. Everything saves itself, always.

Making notes

⌥ ⌘ NNew note from anywhere — it pops up right at your cursor.
Menu bar 📝Click the note icon in your menu bar → New Note.

The top bar of every note

✕Hides the note (never deletes!). Reopen it from All Notes.

Writing & formatting

⌘B ⌘I ⌘UBold, italic, underline — or use the toolbar buttons.
Fonts & sizesSelect some text first, then pick a Font or Size from the toolbar. No selection? It applies to what you type next.
Colored textSelect text, click a color dot in the toolbar.
ChecklistsClick ☑︎ Todo, or just type [] + space at the start of a line. Click a checkbox to tick it off — done items get crossed out.
BulletsClick • List, or type - + space. No ⌥8 nonsense.
TyposFixed as you type — native macOS autocorrect is on in every note.
✏️ DrawClick the pencil in the top bar to sketch right on the note. Pick a pen color, use 🧽 to erase, ✓ Done when finished. Doodles save with the note.
PaperThe ● color menu also sets the paper: Plain, Lined, or Grid.

Tear off a page 📄

Grab the curled corner at the bottom-right and pull toward the middle of the note. As you pull, the page peels back and a fresh note is revealed underneath — pull far enough and the top page tears free entirely, following your cursor so you can drop it anywhere. Let go early and it settles right back down, no new note left behind. Just like peeling pages off a real notepad.
💡 Markdown shortcuts — type these at the start of a line, then space:
- bullet  ·  [] checklist  ·  1. numbered list  ·  # big heading  ·  ## small heading

All Notes board

Menu bar → All Notes… shows every note (including hidden ones) in a grid. Search by name or content, click a card to open it, or use the buttons on each card: open ↗, copy ⧉, delete 🗑. Deleting asks first — it's the only way a note ever dies.
Select lets you delete a batch at once: click cards to check them off, then Delete to remove them all together (still asks first).

Good to know

SavingThere is no save button. Every keystroke is saved automatically, position and all. Quit, restart — notes come back exactly where they were.
Launch at LoginMenu bar → Launch at Login keeps your notes with you after every restart.
ResizingDrag any edge, or the top-left, top-right, and bottom-left corners to resize diagonally. The bottom-right corner is reserved for tearing off a page.
MovingDrag any blank part of the top bar.
